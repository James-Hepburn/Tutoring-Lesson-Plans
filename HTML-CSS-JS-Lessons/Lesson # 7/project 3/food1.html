Lemon Herb Chicken with Asparagus Risotto

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 lemon (juiced and zested)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme (chopped)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 cups asparagus (trimmed and chopped)
  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 4 cups chicken broth (warmed)
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a bowl, combine lemon juice, lemon zest, minced garlic, chopped thyme, salt, and pepper.
  3. Marinate chicken breasts in the mixture for 30 minutes.
  4. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat. Sear chicken on both sides until golden brown.
  5. Transfer chicken to a baking dish and bake for 20-25 minutes or until cooked through.
  6. Meanwhile, in a separate pan, sauté asparagus in butter until tender.
  7. Add Arborio rice to the pan and stir to coat with butter.
  8. Gradually add warmed chicken broth, stirring frequently until the rice is creamy and cooked al dente.
  9. Stir in Parmesan cheese and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. Serve the lemon herb chicken over the asparagus risotto and enjoy!
