Butternut Squash and Sage Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz. pasta (your choice)
  • 2 cups butternut squash (peeled, seeded, and diced)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/4 cup fresh sage leaves
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese (grated)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Crushed red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat.
  3. Add diced butternut squash and cook until tender and slightly caramelized.
  4. Add minced garlic and fresh sage leaves to the skillet; saut√© until fragrant.
  5. Combine cooked pasta with the butternut squash mixture in the skillet.
  6. Season with salt, pepper, and crushed red pepper flakes (if using).
  7. Sprinkle grated Parmesan cheese over the pasta and toss until well combined.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with additional Parmesan and sage leaves if desired.
