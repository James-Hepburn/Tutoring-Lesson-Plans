Treatment

Treatment Tips:

  1. Clean the tattoo gently with mild soap and water.
  2. Apply a thin layer of antibiotic ointment and cover with a sterile bandage.
  3. Avoid exposing the tattoo to direct sunlight or swimming for the first few days.
  4. Keep the tattoo moisturized with unscented lotion.

Safety Health Tips:

  • Ensure the tattoo artist uses sterilized equipment and follows proper hygiene.
  • Follow the aftercare instructions provided by the tattoo artist.
  • Avoid scratching or picking at the tattoo to prevent infections.
  • If you notice any signs of infection, consult a healthcare professional.
