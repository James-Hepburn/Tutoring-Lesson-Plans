Caligula

Reign: 37 AD - 41 AD

Achievements and Accomplishments:

  • Known for his extravagant and controversial rule
  • Initiated large-scale construction projects, including the expansion of the aqueduct system
  • Reformed the currency system and issued new coins
  • Promoted the arts and theater, which flourished during his reign
  • Established the Praetorian Guard as a powerful institution
